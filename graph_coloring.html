Graph Coloring


     ---- INSTRUCTIONS ----

      1.    Color the nodes of the graph with different colors such that no two adjacent nodes have the same color.
      2.   Click consecutively on a node for selection of different colors.
      3.   You have to color the graph with the MINIMUM possible colors.
      4.   Once done,click on the submit button.
      5.   In case of tie, MINIMUM no of moves will be considered.
      6.   Do not refresh the page.
